Burton secured promotion to Sky Bet League One after Lucas Akins scored both goals in their 2-1 win at Morecambe. Jimmy Floyd Hasselbaink's side are up as, despite fourth-placed Bury still being able to catch them, third-placed Wycombe - who have played a game more than the Shakers - can not. Damien McCrory saw his 28th-minute penalty kept out by Andreas Arestidou but just two minutes later Akins slotted through the goalkeeper's legs having been played through by Stuart Beavon.
