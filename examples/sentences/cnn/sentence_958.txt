Last month Instagram launched a standalone app called Layout. It has four main features called Faces, Mirroring, Photo Booth and Previews. Users begin by selecting as many images, up to the maximum of nine, to feature in a collage. Photos can be selected or deselected at any point. As the user adds more images the layout of the collage changes and photos can be dragged between sections and different sized boxes as desired.
