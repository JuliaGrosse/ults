How to Steal a Dog is a book written by Barbara O'Connor. It was written in 2007 and also has a 2014 film adaption.

Plot
Georgina Hayes, her brother Toby, and their parents lived happily in an apartment in Darby, North Carolina until her father leaves their family. Their limited income results in Georgina and her family being evicted from the apartment, and since then, they have lived in their car and in poverty.
