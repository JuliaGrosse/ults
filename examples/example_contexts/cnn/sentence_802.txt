Napoli all but secured their place in the semi-finals of the Europa League after inflicting a devastating defeat on Wolfsburg at the Volkswagen Arena on Thursday night. Gonzalo Higuain and Marek Hamsik put the visitors firmly in control as Rafa Benitez's side struck twice in the opening 45 minutes before the Slovakian midfielder added a third after the break. Substitute Manolo Gabbiadini made it four just moments after replacing two-goal hero Hamisk from the bench before former Arsenal striker Nicklas Bendtner pulled a goal back for the hosts. Manolo Gabbiadini heads Napoli 4-0 ahead moments after coming on as a second-half substitute. Gabbiadini celebrates with team-mate David Lopez after giving the visitors a four-goal advantage. Marek Hamsik celebrates with team-mates after making it 3-0 to Napoli in the first leg of the quarter-final.
