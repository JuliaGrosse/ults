Alarming footage has surfaced of a crowd of street racers threatening the police officers who rescued their friends from a serious crash. Captured at a known hoon hotspot in Yatala on the Gold Coast, the body cam vision shows the officers saving several victims from inside a flipped Holden Commodore despite the threat of smouldering flames on the car. A 20-year-old man has been charged with a medley of offences after he flipped the vehicle on Saturday night in an intense police chase that left one passenger with a broken neck. Officers can be seen extinguishing the flames and administering first aid as they wait for paramedics to transport the injured victims to hospital, according to 9 News.
